## Translator & Intermediary for International Trade
**Company exporting products to Russia**

I worked as a communication bridge between the company and Russian‑speaking clients and partners.
My responsibilities included:

- Translating technical and commercial information (Spanish ↔ Russian ↔ English)
- Facilitating negotiations and clarifying requirements
- Ensuring smooth communication between departments and international clients
- Supporting documentation and export processes

This role improved my ability to communicate clearly, manage expectations and adapt messages to different audiences.

## Night Receptionist – Hotel Environment
**Front desk, customer support & technical troubleshooting**

Working as a night receptionist required handling a wide variety of tasks independently:

- Managing check‑ins, check‑outs and guest requests

- Solving technical issues (TVs, AC units, access systems)
- Maintaining order, cleanliness and safety during night shifts
- Handling unexpected situations with calm and professionalism

This experience strengthened my autonomy, responsibility and ability to solve problems under pressure.
