**UPC School – Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya**
**Final weighted grade: 7.7 / 10**

I completed the MBA for Technology and Industrial Companies at UPC School, a programme designed to strengthen leadership, strategic thinking and innovation capabilities in highly technical and industrial environments.
According to the official programme, the MBA aims to *“train highly qualified leaders capable of directing, innovating and transforming companies within the technological and industrial sector”*.

The programme combines strategic decision‑making, operations management, financial analysis, innovation, marketing, talent management and a final business project.

# Programme Overview

The MBA covers a wide range of business and management areas, including:

- Strategic analysis, competitive positioning and decision‑making under uncertainty
- Leadership, communication, conflict resolution and organisational change
- Innovation methodologies, business model design and intellectual property
- Marketing strategy, digital presence and customer experience
- Operations management, logistics, Lean & Just‑In‑Time principles
- Financial statements, valuation, risk management and funding strategies
- Talent development, organisational culture and HR strategy
- A full business simulation integrating marketing, finance, operations and HR
- A final business project developed and defended before a professional panel
